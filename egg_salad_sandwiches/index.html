Recipe: Egg Salad Sandwiches (simple)

Written By: Mackenzie Nelson

  • Ingredients: 3
  • Steps: 13
  • Total Time: 1 Hour

Ingredients:

  • 8-10 Eggs
  • Mayonaise
  • Mustard

Optional Additions:

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Pickle/Relish

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a pot - put the stove on high
  2. Add 8-10 eggs
  3. Keep water at a boil - cook for 12-15 minutes
  4. Turn off stove - empty water from pot
  5. Grab a glass cup (tall and skinny works best) and a spoon
  6. Using the spoon, place one egg at a time in the glass cup and shake it for a few seconds until you see cracks in the shell
  7. Take the egg out of the glass - the shells should easily slide off with minimum effort
  8. Place shells and unshelled eggs in separate bowls/containers
  9. Once all the eggs are peeled and placed in a bowl/container, add the mayonnaise and the mustard (ratio to the taste of the chef, I prefer a higher mayo to mustard ratio)
  10. Add any additional ingredients you want, some suggestions are listed at below
  11. Mash the ingredients together until the eggs are broken up and everything has mixed evenly together
  12. Do a taste test and make any adjustments
  13. Place in the fridge to cool then ENJOY!!
